Questions

Frequently
Asked Questions

How long do braids last? +
With proper care, our braids typically last 4–8 weeks. Box braids and cornrows can last up to 6 weeks. We'll advise on aftercare during your appointment.
Do I need to bring my own hair? +
No need — we supply extensions for most styles. Just let us know your preferred length and colour when booking on WhatsApp.
Is the plaiting painful? +
We specialise in pain-free styling. We never pull too tight and always check your comfort throughout the session.
How do I book an appointment? +
Simply WhatsApp us! Tell us which service you want, your preferred date and time, and we'll confirm your slot quickly — usually within minutes.
Do you deliver hair products? +
Yes! We offer free delivery within Gaborone for orders over P150. WhatsApp us your order and address and we'll arrange delivery.
How long does a treatment session take? +
Treatment sessions range from 45 minutes (deep conditioning) to 90 minutes (full spa treatment). We'll give you an exact time when booking.
