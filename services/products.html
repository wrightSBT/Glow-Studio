Usage Guide

How to Use Each Product

01

Section Hair

Part your hair into 4–6 sections to expose the scalp.

02

Apply to Scalp

Drop a few drops directly onto the scalp along each parting.

03

Massage In

Use your fingertips to massage in circular motions for 2–3 minutes.

04

Repeat Daily

Use every morning or evening for best results. Do not rinse out.

01

Shampoo First

Start with freshly washed, towel-dried hair.

02

Apply Generously

Work the mask from mid-shaft to ends, avoiding the scalp.

03

Wait 15–30 Min

Cover with a shower cap. Add heat for deeper penetration.

04

Rinse Thoroughly

Rinse with cool water to seal the cuticle and lock in moisture.

01

Wet Hair Fully

Saturate all hair and scalp with warm water.

02

Apply & Lather

Work a palm-sized amount into a gentle lather on the scalp.

03

Cleanse Scalp

Focus on the scalp — the shampoo will clean the lengths as it rinses.

04

Follow with Mask

Always follow with the Deep Repair Mask for best results.
